ABOUT THE GAME

Elementary is a coop rougleike game. Up to three people have to fight through randomly generated levels and defeat the boss of each level. Each player is assigned one of three elements (fire, water and nature). The enemies are made up of the same elements. Fire will deal more damage to nature, just like water to fire and nature with water. This encourages more team play and cooperation.
